Thank you for RSVPing to HopHacks Fall 2022! Below is some important information for our fully in-person hackathon at the Johns Hopkins University campus from September 16th to September 18th (this Friday!).

Location: HopHacks will be held at Johns Hopkins University's Hodson Hall. We’ll have organizers greet you at the front door, and check-in will be next to Hodson 110.

Time: Check-in begins September 16th (Friday) at 6:30PM, and the opening ceremony is at 8:00PM!

Vaccination: Due to university policies, all participants must be vaccinated and boosted in order to attend HopHacks. Please upload your COVID-19 vaccination through your Profile page if you have not done so.

Identification: Please bring a form of ID, such as a school ID, driver’s license, or passport. We will check these during check-in.

Discord: Please join the HopHacks Fall 2022 Discord here. You can get a head start on team forming if needed, and ask questions you may have!

Food: Meals and snacks will be served free of cost for all hackers!

Sleep: Sleeping rooms will be set aside in Hodson Hall. However, hackers will need to bring their own sleeping bags and/or pillows. (JHU students can sleep in their own rooms, of course)

Transportation: Unfortunately, we are no longer offering buses to HopHacks. We apologize for the late notice.

Parking: If you are driving to HopHacks, we will have a limited number of parking passes available for you. Parking can be found in the South Garage (under Mason Hall).

We'll see you soon! Please email us with any questions you have at hophacks2022@gmail.com.
