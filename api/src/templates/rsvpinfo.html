Please join the HopHacks Fall 2025 Discord here for the latest updates and announcements. You can get a head start on team forming if needed, and ask questions you may have!

Food:

Meals and snacks will be served free of cost for all hackers! An email will be sent soon where you can indicate any dietary restrictions.

Identification:

Please bring a form of ID, such as a school ID, driver's license, or passport. We will check these during check-in.

Transportation:

Please fill out the busing form here if you are interested in a bus coming to your school or city! We cannot guarantee busing, but we will aim to let you know a few weeks before the hackathon so you can make alternate arrangements.

Sleep:

Sleeping rooms will be set aside in Hodson Hall. However, hackers will need to bring their own sleeping bags and/or pillows. (JHU students can sleep in their own rooms, of course).

Parking:

If you are driving to HopHacks, we will have a limited number of parking passes available for you. Parking can be found in the South Garage (under Mason Hall).
